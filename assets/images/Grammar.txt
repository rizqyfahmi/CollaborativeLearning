	1. 	Simple Present Tense
		Rumus : S + V(s/es) + O
	2. 	Present Continous Tense
		Rumus : S + am/is/are + Ving
	3. 	Present Prefect
		Rumus : S + has/have + V3
	4.	Present Perfect Continous
		Rumus : S + has/have + been + Ving
	5.	Simple Past Tense
		Rumus : S + V2
	6. 	Past Continous
		Rumus : S + was/were + Ving
	7.	Past Perfect
		Rumus : S + had + V3
	8. 	Past Perfect Continous
		Rumus : S + had + been + Ving
	9.  Simple Future Tense
		Rumus : S + will/shall + V
	10. Future Continous
		Rumus : S + will/shall + be + Ving
	11. Future Perfect
		Rumus : S + will/shall + have + V3
	12. Future Perfect Continous
		Rumus : S + will/shall + have + been + Ving
	13. Past Future Tense
		Rumus : S + would/should + V
	14. Past Future Continous
		Rumus : S + would/should + be + Ving
	15. Past Future Perfect
		Rumus : S + would/should + have + V3
	16. Past Future Perfect Continous
		Rumus : S + would/should + have + been + Ving
